About Us

[Message from owner: Florida was created on the 24th of March, 2026 and I wanted to create Florida after the recent raid of our 1.6k member server. I wanted to give ER:LC another chance for other users and show that it can be enjoyable when RPing with friends and other people.]

Welcome to Florida State Roleplay (FSRP), a thriving ER:LC community dedicated to providing an exciting, realistic, and enjoyable roleplay experience for players of all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned roleplayer or just starting your journey in Emergency Response: Liberty County, our server offers a welcoming environment where everyone can learn, grow, and have fun.

At Florida State Roleplay, we believe that great roleplay comes from a strong community. That's why we focus on creating realistic scenarios, encouraging creativity, and maintaining a friendly atmosphere where members can build memorable experiences together. From routine traffic stops and emergency medical responses to large-scale events and community operations, every roleplay session is designed to feel immersive and engaging.

Our server is supported by a dedicated team of staff members who work hard to ensure that the community remains organized, fair, and enjoyable for all players. We strive to provide professional moderation, quick support when needed, and a fair system that allows everyone to enjoy roleplay without unnecessary disruptions. Our staff team is always looking for ways to improve the server and make the experience even better for our members.

What makes Florida State Roleplay special is our commitment to quality. We encourage realistic roleplay standards while still ensuring that players can relax, have fun, and make lasting friendships. We regularly host community events, training sessions, patrols, and special roleplay scenarios that bring members together and keep the server active and exciting.

Whether you dream of serving as a law enforcement officer, responding to emergencies as a firefighter or paramedic, or creating unique civilian roleplay stories, Florida State Roleplay provides the opportunity to make those experiences come to life. Every member plays an important role in shaping our community and helping it grow.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about Florida State Roleplay. We are proud of the community we have built and look forward to welcoming new members who share our passion for realistic, fun, and professional ER:LC roleplay. Join us today and become part of a community where every roleplay matters and every member counts.

Your story starts here. Welcome to Florida State Roleplay. 🚔🚒🚑🌴
