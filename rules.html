Rules — Florida State Role-Play

Welcome to our rules hub — read carefully and follow them to keep roleplay professional and fair.

1. Welcome to Florida State Role-Play, where we focus on immersive, high-quality roleplay built on realism, professionalism, and community.

To keep this standard, we have clear rules to ensure fairness and an enjoyable experience for everyone, so please take a moment to read and follow them.

Whether you're new or experienced, we’re here to provide an authentic roleplay environment where every story matters.

Underneath you will find our set of rules:

  • ▸ Discord Rules — Maintain a respectful and organized environment.
  • ▸ Game Rules — Ensure realistic and immersive gameplay.
  • ▸ Department Rules — Maintain professionalism across all departments.

In-Game Regulations (1/5)

Below are our rules for the In-Game server. Please ensure you follow all the rules laid out below. Failure to read these rules will result in moderation action. Trying to find "Loopholes" will also result in moderation action.

‣ Rule 1 - Roleplay Permissions & Restrictions

Scenes involving hostages, bank robberies, or jewellery store heists must be approved by staff beforehand regardless of your tier.

Hitman & suicide roleplays are not allowed. Gang roleplay (groups over 4 people committing crimes together) are not permitted.

‣ Rule 2 - Character Avatars

When roleplaying, you must have a realistic avatar. This includes but is not limited to realistic skin tones, realistic accessories, and realistic body shape.

All team members must use respective team uniforms. If you are not roleplaying on the team or as an LEO, you may not wear a corresponding uniform.

For something to be considered a "costume" you must have visible areas of skin. Costumes must be worn with purpose. This also includes vests, which must match your roleplay.

‣ Rule 3 - Driving

All members are expected to properly handle their vehicles. You must follow all traffic laws unless you have a valid roleplay reason to break them. Avoid excessive speeding and dangerous driving.

‣ Rule 4 - Roleplay Rushing

All roleplay actions must be properly carried out. If a person is being treated, you must roleplay the medical process instead of skipping it. Tow rushing is also not allowed.

"-Cuffs-" must be stated in chat before cuffing someone.

In-Game Regulations (3/5)

‣ Rule 5 - Interfering with Roleplay

You may only be involved in a roleplay if you initiated it or are logically part of it. Otherwise, you may not join the scene.

‣ Rule 6 - Metagaming

You may only use information gained within roleplay. You cannot use outside information such as third-person view or scanners to gain an advantage.

‣ Rule 7 - Valuing Life & Fear Roleplay

You must roleplay as a normal individual who values their life and will not take unrealistic risks that endanger themselves.

In-Game Regulations (4/5)

‣ Rule 8 - Civilian Jobs

Construction vehicles must remain on-site unless getting fuel. DOT may transport them for roadwork.

Tractors must primarily remain in fields and only leave for logical reasons such as fuel or equipment.

‣ Rule 9 - Deathmatching

Random deathmatching is strictly prohibited. You must have a valid roleplay reason to cause harm.

Vehicle deathmatching is also prohibited. Using vehicles to damage players or surroundings without RP reason is not allowed.

In-Game Regulations (5/5)

‣ Rule 10 - Roleplay Restrictions

Departments have their own rules for vehicles, items, and actions. Failure to follow them may result in removal.

‣ Rule 11 - Safe Zones

Safe zones include civilian spawns, DOT, FD, PD, Sheriff’s Office, Jail, and Hospital. No crimes may start here.

‣ Rule 12 - Roleplay Quality

This is a serious roleplay server. All roleplay must be realistic and have context. No pointless shootouts or overreactions. Avoid repetitive scenarios.

‣ Rule 14 - Use Common Sense

Do not do unrealistic or absurd actions. Examples include pulling large weapons from nowhere, claiming invisible equipment, faking severe injuries unrealistically, making absurd demands or scenarios.

Discord Regulations (1/4)

All the rules/guidelines/information applies to profile pictures, usernames, statuses, banners, voice channels & direct messages between members of this Discord. If you consistently act in bad faith in a way that is not explicitly stated to be against the rules, we will still take action against you. We do not moderate direct messages unless if they impact our server indirectly.

‣ Rule 1 - Pinging.

Members are not permitted to ping Staff roles that are Management + or anybody that possesses the VIP or PRC Official role. Members should also avoid spam pinging or pinging people without reason.

‣ Rule 2 - Discord Nickname.

In the Discord, your server nickname may only consist of your Roblox username. It may contain a callsign if you are roleplaying on a team that needs one. Your name cannot contain "CIV" or any special characters (!,$,*) or fonts.

Discord Regulations (2/4)

‣ Rule 3 - Respect all.

We have zero tolerance for racism, ableism, threats, sexism, homophobia, or discrimination. Disagreements happen, but always be kind and understanding. If someone is rude, don't engage—let moderators handle it.

‣ Rule 4 - Advertising.

Advertising is not allowed. Do not ask for Discord Nitro, server boosts, or unauthorized programs. You may not request or provide in-game currency, items, or services.

Discord Regulations (3/4)

‣ Rule 5 - NSFW/L.

Please avoid posting content that is NSFW/L. This includes but is not limited to content that is only appropriate for members 18+, depicts violence, extremism, or endangers others.

‣ Rule 6 - Alt Accounts.

Unapproved user bots and alternate accounts are not allowed. This includes using accounts to bypass punishments.

‣ Rule 7 - Misuse Of Channels.

Each channel must be used for its intended purpose. Misuse will result in moderation.

Discord Regulations (4/4)

‣ Rule 8 - English Only.

All communication must be in modern English.

‣ Rule 9 - Spam or Malicious Content.

Posting repeated content across channels is not allowed. Do not send malicious links, IP loggers, malware, scams, or harmful content.

‣ Rule 10 - Dating.

This server is not for dating or romantic relationships. Respect boundaries and avoid inappropriate behavior.

Department Regulations (1/3)

Florida Police Department (LAPD)

Officers must drive responsibly. Traffic laws may only be broken when lights and sirens are active.

Arrests must be realistic and based on proper roleplay. Random cuffing or detaining is not allowed.

LAPD jurisdiction is limited to the city. Leaving jurisdiction is only permitted during active pursuits or high-priority calls.

In hostage situations, officers must act realistically. Do not rush or immediately use lethal force without proper escalation.

California Highway Patrol (CHP)

Officers must follow proper driving standards and use lights and sirens when engaging in emergency driving.

CHP has jurisdiction across the entire map and is responsible for highways and major incidents.

Department Regulations (2/3)

Florida State Sheriff's Department (FSSD)

Deputies must drive responsibly and only break traffic laws with lights and sirens activated.

Jurisdiction covers county areas. Deputies may enter city limits when assisting or during active pursuits.

Florida rules.

All personnel must respond professionally and use emergency equipment when necessary.

Fire and medical scenes must be handled realistically, including proper equipment usage and coordination.

Department Regulations (3/3)

California Department of Transportation (Caltrans)

Members must follow traffic laws and drive responsibly at all times.

Responsibilities include traffic control, road safety, and assisting scenes when appropriate.

General Department Rules

Whitelisted (WL) vehicles, uniforms, and teams are restricted to authorized members only.

Jurisdiction rules must always be followed unless in active pursuits or major scenarios.

Realism and professionalism are required at all times. Supervisors and staff have final authority in all situations.
